How to use

  1. Pick a pair from the list.
  2. Check the trend direction (EMA200 on 15m/1h is the baseline).
  3. Look for confluence: structure + levels + volume.
  4. Decide only after you confirm on the chart.

Good context

  • Price is above EMA200 and volume grows on pullbacks.
  • Clear higher highs / higher lows with fresh demand zones.
  • Multiple timeframes point the same way.

Avoid

  • Counter-trend entries without confirmation.
  • Single volume spikes with no follow-through.
  • Low-liquidity pairs.
